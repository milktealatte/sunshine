So, the basic premise of the episode is that both groups are heading off on a school excursion. They finally arrive at the place and it really looks like your typical school-excursion-accommodation, BUT... they're going to have to complete a mission in order to win their lunch! Their reaction to this news?



Ravn: We can't eat this time either?

Seoho: No waaaay

Yonghoon: Please don't do this to us!



Then the boys are told even worse news - they have to pick one of the 'hidden games' to compete in, and then ONLY the winning team will get to eat from the lunch buffet.


Ravn explains that the 'Party Hat Penalty Shoot Out' was the game chosen.



Ravn: We had to use a party hat, and the hole at the top was so small you couldn't see anything through it!


All the boys chime in.



Cya: Wow, amazing.... How do you see with this thing?

Harin: This is on a microscopic level.



Then, the penalty shoot out begins!
First up is ONEUS' Leedo. Hwanwoong shouts encouragement.



Hwanwoong: Just... BANG! Just kick it!



Leedo actually manages to kick the ball at the goal! But it's saved by Cya. The screen says "Saved by our goalkeeper maknae Cya!"
The boys regroup, and someone asks Younghoon if he used to be a soccer player.


Younghoon: I used to play midfield.


It's goal keeper Hwanwoong vs midfielder Younghoon. Younghoon receives a lot of support from the ONEWE boys.
