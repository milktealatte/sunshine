ONEUS Ravn's Wonderful Birthday Present to Fans - Cover of '어떻게 지내 (Fall)





Ravn - 어떻게 지내 (Fall)


Guys! It was Ravn's birthday on September 2nd! Happy Birthday Ravn! ^_^

A lot of fans are saying 'thank you' to Ravn, not just for being born, but because he uploaded a new song to his Soundcloud on his birthday (the man never stops working).

Ravn’s latest Soundcloud upload is a cover of 어떻게 지내 (Fall) by Crush. This explains why he put #fall in the hashtags, and also maybe because he recorded it in the last days of the 2018 Korean summer, so he was anticipating the coming of Autumn/Fall? Perhaps? In the notes it says that he both mixed it and performed it himself (so talented!)

This is a beautiful song and shows that Ravn can sing very emotionally-heavy material with real heart.

Note that Ravn only recorded the first verse and chorus. I was waiting for him to say the English line at the end of the chorus, the ‘Don’t wanna fall in love’ just to hear his English-speaking/singing voice… but alas, he ended the recording before then.
